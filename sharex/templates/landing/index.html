Who we are

The fleet

  • The fleet philosophy
  • The pirates

Entrepreneurs are known to travel unchartered and stormy waters - adventerous, unafraid and restless. They have a goal, but are not always sure how to navigate to get there. In the midst of a storm, the entrepreneur will benefit from the help and support from other pirates sailing the same waters.

The fleets mission is to bring together entrepreneurs as pirates to share their knowledge, experiences and resources, to reach individual goals - together.

We aim to get all aboard. As the ships come together we will accelerate the wind in our sailes and find a steady course!

Our value props

why be a pirate?

Learn from fellow pirates

Do you ever have questions related to your business or pirate life - but don’t know who to ask? In our Q&A section you can direct your questions in open areas or within categories, and receive answers from pirates who have navigated the same situations and seas before.

Trade resources

Got some spare room on your ship or maybe your server? Make it available for another pirate and exchange favours. Or if you’re visiting another harbour, why not stay the night with another pirate or work in their office space.

Be captain Bootstrap

Some expenses are the same whether your ship is small or huge. You’ll need office supplies, computers, phone subscriptions and air plane travels. We provide you with discount deals to avoid the sharks and reduce your expenses.

Share your war stories

Want to share or bounce off your thoughts and ideas on business development? In the jungle of blog spaces it’s hard to know which one to choose and where to distribute. Instead, share your stories with other pirates, safe at sea.
